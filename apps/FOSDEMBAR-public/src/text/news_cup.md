### Cerkinfo 2025 Eco-Cup

**New this year**, we are FINALLY selling reusable cups! They've been specially designed for this edition, and we hope you'll enjoy the nostalgia of retro games! You'll be able to let your beer breathe during your tasting.

They *cost € 1.5* and *hold 33 CL* (enough for Cerkinfo beers).

Don't hesitate to ask for one at the bar when you order, and **take it with you throughout the event**.
Please also take care not to throw them away anywhere, as their ecological purpose loses all meaning...
