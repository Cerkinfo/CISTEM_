### How to find us

We have 5 sales outlets spread across the campus:
- *Bar Campouce* : [Building F1 2nd Floor] With a direct view of the lawn, you can enjoy our full food and drink menu, while chatting and enjoying the tables at your disposal.
- *Bar Chez Théo* : [Building F1 2nd Floor] The bar with the most space, the view is less spectacular, but ideal for large groups. Here, too, you can enjoy our full menu, but note that the bar and coffee stand are a long way away.
- *Bar Foyer* : [Building F1 1nd Floor] A slightly cosier atmosphere, where you can clear your head with our food and drinks. But we don't have our own coffee stand here.
- *Bar Hall U.D* : [Building U Door D 2nd Floor] No time to order in the cafeterias or too long a queue? The bar is coming to you! You'll be able to choose from our full food and drink menu, or of course coffee.
- *Hall K* : [Building K 1st Floor next to the merch] Need a bottle of water before you go to the conference? Or feeling thirsty in the midst of all those FOSDEM merchs purchases? Our “water stand” is there to help you out with snacks and soft drinks!
